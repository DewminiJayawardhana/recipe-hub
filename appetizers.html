Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Toast the baguette slices until golden brown.
  2. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, minced garlic, chopped basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Top each toasted baguette slice with the tomato mixture.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and finely chop the stems.
  3. In a bowl, mix chopped stems, cream cheese, garlic, Parmesan, parsley, bread crumbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff each mushroom cap with the mixture.
  5. Place stuffed mushrooms on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
