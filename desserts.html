Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup (165g) packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (350g) chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually add to the creamed mixture.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  8. Cool on wire racks and enjoy!

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 2 kiwis, peeled and diced
  • 1 orange, peeled and sectioned
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp fresh lime juice

Steps:

  1. Combine strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, and orange in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey and lime juice.
  3. Pour dressing over fruit and toss gently to coat.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve cold and enjoy a refreshing dessert!
